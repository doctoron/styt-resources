Three things worth sitting with

Strike 01

AI doesn't replace thinking—it exposes weak thinking.

The fear isn't that AI will take your job. The fear is that it will make visible what was already fragile. That's not a threat. It's a diagnosis. And a diagnosis is where clarity begins.

Strike 02

You're not competing with AI—you're being reshaped by it.

Competition implies you could win or lose. This is different. AI is already changing the frame of what your work means, what your judgment is worth, and how decisions get made around you. The question isn't whether to engage. It's whether to do it consciously.

Strike 03

What AI is actually doing to your decisions.

It's not replacing your decisions—it's reshaping the inputs, the speed, and the confidence behind them. You're still deciding. But the conditions have changed. Knowing how is the practical edge most people don't yet have.
